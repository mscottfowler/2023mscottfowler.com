A Fayetteville, Arkansas-based acrylic and watercolor artist

A graduate of the Southwest University of Visual Arts, I have an Applied Science degree in advertising art. His varied career has gone through several changes from newspapers and graphic design to web development and now fine art.

A native of Arkansas, I was raised in an artistic family with a mother who was also an artist. He is a member of the Artists of Northwest Arkansas (ANA) and SPECTRA, and his work can often be found in the gallery at the ANA Experimental House in Rogers, AR.

I also lead the NW Arkansas Chapter of the Interaction Design Foundation (IxDF) where UX professionals network together to advance their careers.
